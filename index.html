Welcome to David Gynn Memorials

A family-run memorial masonry based in Launceston, Cornwall — David started the business over 40 years ago. Today Harry & Eddie continue the craft with the same care and attention, serving churches and cemeteries across the region.

MEMORIALS

Handcrafted memorials, modern service

We design, supply and install memorials using Delabole and Welsh slate and a selection of light greys in granite. From simple tablets to traditional headstones, we manage every step — site checks, parish applications, design approvals and sympathetic installation.

We aim to make the process clear and respectful. If you have an old stone we can re-use or refurbish it; we also offer repainting and lettering services to restore inscriptions.

What we do

Memorials

Design and install bespoke memorials from Delabole and Welsh slate and light granite. We check parish rules and provide full guidance.

How we do it

We work with warmth and care. Our approach is customer-led: we listen first and guide families through design and parish requirements without pressure.
