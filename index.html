Headstones | Tablets & Vases | Inscriptions | Refurbishing

Headstones

We supply and install a wide range of memorial headstones. Our most commonly used materials are black and light-to-dark grey granites, chosen for their durability, finish and long-term appearance. Welsh slate is available for clients who prefer a textured, classic finish.

Note: Delabole slate is rarely used today due to limited availability; we will always advise on suitable alternatives if requested.

We handle parish checks and installation — expect lead times for ordering stone (often several months). We’ll advise on settling times and any site-specific restrictions.

Tablets & Vases

Tablets and vases are commonly supplied in Welsh slate and granite. Vases are made to be durable and weather resistant, with matching finishes to memorials where required. We can supply bespoke shapes and sizes.

Inscriptions

We provide crisp sandblasted and machine-cut lettering, designed to match the style of the stone and the family’s wishes. Repainting is offered where the stone accepts repainting — Welsh slate often accepts repainting better than some foreign slates.

Refurbishing & Cleaning

We offer careful cleaning, repainting and re-setting to restore memorials while respecting their original character. For older stones we recommend conservation-friendly methods and will advise on the best approach for longevity.

Signs & Engraving

We produce slate house signs, plaques and bespoke engraved items. Typical materials include Welsh slate and selected granites for more durable plaques. We also offer engraving of stones that customers bring to our workshop.

In future we plan to offer a simple slate sign designer tool that allows customers to mock up text, choose fonts, and preview colours on mobile devices — this is a planned feature and will be added when ready.
