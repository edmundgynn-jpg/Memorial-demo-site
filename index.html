Memorials — materials & guidance

We supply and install a wide range of memorials, designed to honour loved ones with dignity and care. Our most commonly used materials are black and light-to-dark grey granites — chosen for their durability and refined finish.

We also work with Welsh slate where a classic, textured finish is preferred. Delabole slate is an option only rarely today due to limited availability; we will advise on suitable alternatives where needed.

Each memorial is personalised with inscriptions, motifs and finishes. We handle parish checks and approvals where required, and will advise on realistic lead times (ordering stone can take several months).
