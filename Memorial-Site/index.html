A Family Tradition of Excellence

With over 40 years in the trade, David Gynn Memorials is a family-run business based in Launceston, Cornwall. Founded after David’s decade of experience in monumental masonry, the company is now carried forward by his sons Harry and Eddie. We serve churches, cemeteries, and families across Cornwall and Devon, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques.
